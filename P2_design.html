our Client

Our client is Andrés Jaime who owns two food trucks, called Andrés' bananas!
Andrés Jaime is a Colombian student in Norway, and he is living here without a scholarship. Andrés his food trucks to support himself financially during studies. Here he serves traditional Colombian food.

Purpose

The purpose of the project for Andres’ Bananas! food trucks, is to help Andrés business to grow by giving him a website that's gripping and clear. To promote his brand and provide possible customers with enough information to know what his food truck provides, its history and how to order food from his food truck.

Therefore, this site will provide the user with a menu, an option for ordering food and the location of the food trucks. There will also be information about Colombian food and history.

Target Group

Seeing how there are two trucks, located at Gløshaugen and Kalvkinnet, the audience will mainly be students and employees of NTNU.

The Website

  • Menu The website shall include a menu which displays all of the food options at Andrés' Bananas! and a link to order the food from the menu.
  • Ordering A section or page for ordering the food that is served at Andre's Bananas!
  • About Andrés' Bananas! There will be a section with information about the store, it's goals and the history of Andrés.
  • Colombian Food and History Users will be able to read about traditional Colombian food and its history. Mainly the dishes that are served at Andrés' food truck, such as Patacones.
  • Contact A way of contacting Andrés' Bananas!, either through a form, email or a telephone number

Functional Requirements

  • The website shall accept orders from customers
  • Provide customers with a time for when the food is ready
  • A map with the position of the two food trucks
  • Provide a fallback image for videos
  • A navigation menu for the website

Non-functional Requirements

  • The pages shall load in less than 1 second
  • Have a responsive design, for mobile and desktop
  • Be able to access all pages at all times
  • Order food in five steps or less

Final Location

After the project is finished, the URL for this website will be: andresbananas.no and Andrés will hire someone to maintain the website.
